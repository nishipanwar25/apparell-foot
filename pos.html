Powerful POS Meets Smart Billing. All in One ERP.

From quick retail transactions to full invoicing integration—our cloud POS ensures smooth, fast, and trackable billing operations across your business.

What Is POS in ERP-and Why It Matters

A Point of Sale (POS) system is the digital register where sales happen. In a billing ERP like Fusorum, the POS isn’t a standalone app—it’s tightly integrated with invoicing, tax, inventory, customer management, and accounting.

Whether you’re running a retail store, a restaurant, a pharmacy, or a multi-branch outlet, Fusorum’s POS enables seamless:

  • Order processing & checkout
  • Automated invoice generation
  • Real-time inventory updates
  • Instant GST or VAT calculations
  • Secure payment handling
  • Integration with backend accounting

How Fusorum POS Works?

Fusorum POS is tailored for diverse industries:

  1. Customer Checkout → Products scanned or selected
  2. POS Interface → Auto-calculates taxes, discounts, loyalty
  3. Instant Billing → Invoice generated & synced to ERP
  4. Inventory Adjusted → Stock levels auto-updated
  5. Payment Processed → Multiple modes: UPI, card, cash
  6. Sales Recorded → Centralized reports in dashboard

Key Features of Fusorum’s Integrated POS

  • 💳 Multi-Mode Payments — Accept UPI, credit/debit, wallets, cash
  • 🧾 Real-Time Invoicing — Print/email GST-compliant bills instantly
  • 🗃️ Inventory Sync — Automatically updates stock per sale
  • 🛒 Barcode Scanning — Fast and accurate product search
  • 📶 Offline Mode — Works even with no internet; syncs later
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 User Roles — Cashier, Manager, Owner access levels
  • 🛍️ Multi-Store Support — Run POS across multiple branches
  • 📈 Sales Analytics — Daily summaries, item-wise reports, peak hour tracking
